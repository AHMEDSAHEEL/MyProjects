CaseMate
Android App
An Interactive android application to speed up the ongoing crime investigations in the police department. Scope of the project is to bridge the gap between police department and the people in vicinity of the crime that has taken place so that evidences or proofs can be provide by the local people anonymously in order to speed up the police investigative process
